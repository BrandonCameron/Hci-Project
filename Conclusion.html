Conclusion

As i expected Youtube is a very tightly construted website.
There were no glaring errors i could find and the few errors i found
were very minor. These errors as stuff that can easily be improved
upon which is something that is happening constantly on Youtube.
I found the website to be mostly accesable. The main problem is that
you tab through the side tab on the home page before videos but that is
most likely for the best since youtube videos are constanly loading as you
scroll. Overall I found the usability of Youtube to be exceptional. The
website was quiclky picked up on by users with no experience even though
there is always room for improvement. The website being as big as it
is and used by so many people means that the people at Youtube would have
plenty of data which they have used to improve the website and which they
will use to continue to improve.

accessability for Youtube
